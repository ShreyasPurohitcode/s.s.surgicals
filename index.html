OUR STORY

Our team comes with the experience and knowledge

We are a company with a mission to supply hospitals and clinic care centers with the best equipments for surgical operations and other care systems. S.S.Surgicals is dedicated to providing the best available medical equipment, primarily for the operating room market. “Best available” implies that our equipment must provide superior features for the intended use, ease of operation (ergonomics) and service, high reliability and the lowest acquisition and operating costs.

Our journey is to fully satisfy our customer’s requirements through a process of continuous improvement. It is critical to understand that this is not a short-term program. It’s a long-term commitment aimed at continuously improving the way we work, providing a safe work environment, managing our business processes, selecting quality suppliers, and maintaining clear internal and external communications. It is our goal to position our company for market expansion, thereby providing improved job security and quality of life for all.

We started our journey in 2015. We've provided surgical accessories to big organizations like Yashoda hospital and GIMs medical university. We operate 12 stores in the district of Kalaburagi.Our 'click and pick' service allows customers to place orders directly online for collection at their preferred store at a convenient time. Our customers also have the option to return any items purchased through our online channel at their preferred stores, subject to the standard terms and conditions.
